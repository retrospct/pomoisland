05 — Notch-native progress · the camera is opaque
The hardware notch blocks anything drawn over it — so the timer can't live on the camera. These keep it legible: drop the readout below the notch, or trace progress along the notch's own outline.
Why
The camera housing is real, opaque hardware. UI either sits below it or hugs its outline — never on top.
